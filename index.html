About Me

I am Dr. Arjun Mehta, a board-certified neurosurgeon with over 10 years of extensive experience in advanced neurological care. Trained at AIIMS, Delhi, with specialized fellowships at leading international centers, I focus on complex brain and spinal surgeries, including tumor resections, minimally invasive procedures, and spinal deformity corrections.

Throughout my career, I have performed over 1200 successful surgeries, integrating precision medicine, cutting-edge technology, and patient-centered care to achieve the best possible outcomes. My approach combines surgical excellence with empathy, ensuring that every patient and their family receives comprehensive guidance, reassurance, and support.

Passionate about advancing neurosurgical knowledge, I regularly contribute to international research journals, present at global conferences, and mentor the next generation of neurosurgeons.

Specializations

Brain Surgery

Comprehensive care for complex brain conditions, including tumor resections, aneurysms, epilepsy surgery, and trauma management, using advanced microsurgical techniques for optimal outcomes.

Spine Surgery

Expert management of spinal injuries, degenerative disc disease, and deformities with precision procedures such as spinal fusion, disc replacement, and minimally invasive corrections.

Minimally Invasive Neurosurgery

Endoscopic and microsurgical approaches that reduce surgical trauma, accelerate recovery, and lower complication rates, while maintaining the highest standards of patient safety.

Neurological Consultation & Care

Comprehensive diagnostic evaluation, personalized treatment planning, and long-term follow-up for neurological disorders, ensuring patient-centered care throughout the treatment journey.

Pediatric Neurosurgery

Specialized care for neurological disorders in children, including congenital malformations, tumors, and epilepsy, with gentle, minimally invasive techniques tailored to young patients.

Neurorehabilitation

Personalized rehabilitation programs to restore function and quality of life following neurological surgery, stroke, or spinal injury, combining therapy, monitoring, and patient education.
